01 · Payments

Paying for your launch.

Deposits, installments, and how money actually moves between you and Orbitra.

How do I pay Orbitra?

Two ways: MCB Juice send-to-mobile to our business Juice number, or a direct bank transfer to our registered Mauritian business account. Payment details are in the scope document you receive at signing. No cards, no international processors — just the methods you already use.

How are installments structured?

Every launch package has a fixed deposit (40% of the package price) that starts the work. The balance is split over one or two installments tied to delivery milestones — never open-ended, never negotiated mid-project. You see the full schedule before signing.

Is the deposit refundable?

Yes — within 7 days of signing, if work has not yet begun. Once we start discovery, the deposit is non-refundable. Installments tied to completed milestones are also non-refundable. We're transparent about this upfront so there are no surprises.

What happens if I miss an installment?

Work pauses until the installment is settled. We don't add penalties or interest. If something's changed on your side, talk to us — we'd rather adjust the timeline than create friction. We're building a long relationship, not chasing a deadline.

Why don't you accept card payments?

Our clients overwhelmingly prefer Juice and bank transfer — they're familiar, fast, and don't add processor fees onto your price. Keeping payment local also keeps the relationship local.

02 · Ecommerce checkout

How your store will accept payments.

This is separate from how you pay Orbitra. Here we mean how your end-customers pay you, at checkout, on the Shopify store we build.

What payment options do you configure at checkout?

Every ecommerce package — Lite, Starter, Full, and Premium — ships with three checkout options for your customers: Stripe, MIPS, and MCB Juice send-to-mobile. That covers international cards, local cards via MIPS, and the Juice-first buyers who'd rather not enter card details at all.

Do I need separate accounts with Stripe and MIPS?

Yes — both are your accounts, in your business name, receiving payments directly. We walk you through the setup during the launch and connect them to your Shopify store. You keep full control of payouts and reconciliation.

What does the Shopify subscription cost?

Shopify is Rs 1,600/month, paid directly to Shopify (not to Orbitra). This is separate from the launch package and from the optional Care Plan. We disclose this on every ecommerce package card so there's no ambiguity about ongoing costs.

Can I add more payment methods later?

Yes. Shopify supports a wide range of providers, and additional integrations can be configured as an add-on (Rs 3,500 per app integration beyond the defaults). Most clients launch with the three defaults and find them sufficient.

03 · Scope

What's in, what's out, and why.

We sell defined packages, not custom projects. That's the whole model — and the reason we can quote installments instead of hourly rates.

Why packages instead of custom quotes?

Custom quotes are how small projects become long, expensive, and unfinished. With fixed packages, you know exactly what you're getting, when, and for how much — and we know exactly what to deliver. It's better for both of us.

What's included in every launch?

Every package includes the website, the branding work appropriate to that tier, training calls, a written usage guide, and post-launch WhatsApp support. Service packages also include social profile setup; Full and Premium include a full month of social content production.

What's not included?

Unlimited revisions, undefined creative direction, copywriting beyond your supplied material (unless explicitly in scope), product photography (available as an add-on), or work outside the package definition. If you want something we don't sell, we'll tell you — and often point you to someone who does.

What if I need a small change after launch?

If you're on a Care Plan, up to two minor updates per month are included. Without a Care Plan, one-off support is available at Rs 1,500/hour, 30-minute minimum block. Larger changes — adding pages, a booking system, or an ecommerce module — are quoted as upsell packages at fixed prices.

What if my project genuinely doesn't fit any package?

Then we're probably not the right partner — and we'll say so. Highly experimental briefs, undefined business ideas, and bespoke creative direction are outside our model. The audit will surface this in the first few minutes, so nobody wastes time.

04 · Care Plan

Keeping your site running, on your terms.

The Care Plan is optional. Strongly recommended, but always optional. Here's how it works.

What does the Care Plan cover?

Hosting, domain, and business email kept active. Daily backups and security monitoring. Up to two minor content updates per month (additional updates at Rs 1,500 each, billed transparently). A monthly performance snapshot. The same coverage applies across all four commitment tiers — only the price changes.

What are the commitment tiers and prices?

Four tiers. Longer commitments unlock real savings — never lock-in tactics.

  • Monthly · no commitmentRs 3,500/mo
  • Quarterly · ~10% savedRs 9,500/quarter
  • Semi-annual · ~17% savedRs 17,500/6 months
  • Annual · ~24% savedRs 32,000/year
Bundle the first year with your launch package and the annual Care Plan is Rs 28,000 — a one-time, point-of-launch discount of Rs 4,000. After year one, it renews at standard tier pricing.

Can I cancel?

Yes, on every tier. Monthly: cancel any time with 30 days notice. Quarterly and semi-annual: cancel at end of term with 14 days notice. Annual: cancel at end of term with 30 days notice. You can also upgrade to a longer commitment at any time, with the price difference prorated.

What if I'd rather self-manage?

Perfectly valid. Every launch hands over full admin access to your WordPress or Shopify dashboard, hosting and domain credentials transferred into your name, a step-by-step manual for common updates, and recordings of your training calls. You own everything we built.

What won't you do without a Care Plan?

We won't make updates on your behalf, monitor your site for downtime or security issues, handle renewals for hosting, domain, or email, or restore your site if something breaks. If you need help later, one-off support is available at Rs 1,500/hour, 30-minute minimum.

Can I subscribe later?

Yes — any time, starting from any tier, no penalty for previously declining. Many clients self-manage for the first few months and add a Care Plan once their business picks up pace.
