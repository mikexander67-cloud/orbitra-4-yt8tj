About Orbitra

Built for Mauritian small businesses, by someone who gets where you're starting from.

Orbitra is a structured launch agency for early-stage and growing Mauritian businesses. We build the website, the branding, and your first month of social — and we teach you how to use it. Defined packages. Fixed prices. Payments over two to three months.

How we think

A different shape of agency.

Most small businesses in Mauritius sit between two options that don't quite fit. We built Orbitra to be the third.

Freelancers are affordable, but the work arrives in pieces and you're left to stitch it together. Full agencies deliver everything, but the price puts them out of reach for a business that's just getting started.

Orbitra sells launch readiness. Not hours, not retainers, not custom briefs. A complete package — website, branding, social setup, and the training to operate it — delivered as a defined scope with a fixed price and clear installments.

We work with people launching their first real digital presence. That means we move calmly, explain everything, and hand over a business you can actually run on your own after we're done.

  1. 01

    Fixed packages, not quotes.

    You see the price before you talk to us. Nothing is negotiated, nothing is hidden.

  2. 02

    Installments, not lump sums.

    Deposit to start, balance across milestones. Paid via MCB Juice or bank transfer.

  3. 03

    Education baked in.

    Training calls, a written manual, and WhatsApp support so you can run what we build.

  4. 04

    Honest about ongoing costs.

    The Care Plan is optional. Self-management is a real option. You decide what fits.

Who we work with

A good fit, and a fair warning.

We'd rather be honest upfront than disappoint you halfway through.

This is for you if…

  • You're launching your first real digital presence, or replacing something that never worked.
  • You run a service business, small product brand, or owner-operated company in Mauritius.
  • You want a clear price and a clear timeline, not an open-ended quote.
  • You're ready to pay a deposit upfront and the balance across milestones.
  • You'd rather be guided through a defined package than design one from scratch.
  • You want to learn how to run your own site after launch.

This is not for you if…

  • You want a highly experimental, one-off creative brief outside our packages.
  • Your business idea is still undefined and you need someone to shape it for you.
  • You can't pay a deposit before work begins.
  • You expect unlimited revisions or fully bespoke creative direction.
  • You're looking for the cheapest possible option above all else.
  • You want indefinite monthly retainers rather than a fixed scope.
