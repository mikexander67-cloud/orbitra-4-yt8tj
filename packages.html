If you'd rather self-manage

That's a real option. Every package hands over full admin access, your hosting and domain credentials, a step-by-step manual, and recordings of your training calls. You can also book one-off support sessions at Rs 1,500/hour, 30-minute minimum, whenever you need a hand.

Without an active Care Plan, Orbitra won't make updates on your behalf, monitor your site, handle renewals, or restore your site if something breaks. You can subscribe to the Care Plan at any time, starting from any tier — no penalty for previously declining.
